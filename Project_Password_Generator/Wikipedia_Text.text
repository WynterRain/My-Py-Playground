Bubble tea (also known as pearl milk tea, 
bubble milk tea, tapioca milk tea, or boba tea or boba; 
Bubble Tea is a tea-based drink that originated in 
Taiwan in the early 1980s.It most commonly 
consists of tea accompanied by chewy tapioca balls 
("boba" or "pearls"), but it can be made with other toppings as well.
Bubble tea has many varieties and flavors, 
but the two most popular varieties are black pearl milk tea 
and green pearl milk tea 
("pearl" signifies the tapioca balls at the bottom).
